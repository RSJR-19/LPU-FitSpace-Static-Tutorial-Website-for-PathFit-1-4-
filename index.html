About FitSpace

The Physical Education Department of Lyceum of the Philippines University – Cavite fosters lifelong fitness, discipline, and holistic wellness among Lyceans. It manages the PathFit 1–4 programs, promoting active living through academic and practical experiences.

LPU Fit Space

LPU FitSpace is a digital hub for PATHFIT lessons, videos, and resources, designed to supplement classroom learning and support holistic student growth.
