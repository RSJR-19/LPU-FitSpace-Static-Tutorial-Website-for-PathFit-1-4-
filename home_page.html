About FitSpace

What is FitSpace?

Our Purpose

LPU FitSpace is a digital hub for PATHFIT lessons, videos, and resources, designed to supplement classroom learning and support holistic student growth.

Our Goal

To provide a centralized, user-friendly platform for PATHFIT curriculum, making physical education accessible and engaging for all students.

Why Study at FitSpace?

Centralized Learning

Access all PATHFIT lessons in one place, with no need to search through multiple sources.

Clean Layouts

Lessons are presented with modern design principles for easy understanding and retention.

Quick Access

Find PATHFIT content quickly, supporting both classroom and independent study.

PATHFIT Modules

PATHFIT 1

Movement Competency Training: Foundational PE principles, movement skills, and body control.

PATHFIT 2

Exercise-Based Fitness: Physical training, exercise planning, and fitness measurement.

PATHFIT 3 & 4

Applied Physical Activities: Performance-based and recreational activities for skill application.
