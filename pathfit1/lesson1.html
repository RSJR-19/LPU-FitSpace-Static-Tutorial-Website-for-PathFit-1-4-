Introduction to Physical Education

Physical Education (P.E.) is an educational course that focuses on the development of physical fitness and the ability to perform and enjoy day-to-day physical activities. It plays a vital role in promoting overall wellness and encouraging an active lifestyle.

Exercises

  • Reflect on why physical activity is essential in your daily life.
  • List down your 3 favorite physical activities and their benefits.
